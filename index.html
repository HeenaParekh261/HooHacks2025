Camping Safety Tips

  • Don't approach bears, and if you do, don't make unBEARable jokes
  • Don't drink water from streams, lakes or rivers without filtering it
  • Don't wander away from your group if you are traveling with a group
  • Make sure to be aware of your surroundings, and if you sense something unsafe, go back to safety
